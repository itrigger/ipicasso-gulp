Paint by numbers sets Ipicasso.co.uk – get closer to art!

Welcome to the specialized online-store which is fully dedicated to interesting and exciting hobby - paint by numbers.

Paint by numbers sets are classified as DIY goods (DO IT YOURSELF). Originally invented more than 50 years ago in the USA, this type of creativity is gaining momentum and is one of the main drivers of the modern market of DIY products, experiencing rapid growth and gaining more and more new fans around the world.

Coloring by numbers is a unique opportunity for everyone to feel like an artist, creating a masterpiece with their own hands. Thanks to the paint by numbers technique, you can create beautiful paintings even without drawing skills!

The technique of painting by numbers is simple and accessible to everybody.
